# Foundation

Learn about the visual foundation of this Deesign System, and the core principles we include in our designs.

## Overview

### Visual foundation

A great user experience is comprised of more than components and individual patterns. This visual foundation includes our color palettes, iconography, use of spacing, motion, and more.

### Core principles

Core principles are a set of principles in our designs. You can find out more about our approach to accessibility and responsive design in their respective articles.
